05
Documentary · Film · Interview

Antiques in Time Documentary

+

An in-depth documentary exploring the life of John Shantz, proprietor of a refined watch restoration business in Stratford, Ontario. Established in 1977, the company has been a haven for mechanical watches, protecting not only fine artistry but the histories attached to them. Through interviews with John and his customers, the film touches on tradition, preservation, and the emotional connection people form with intricate machines from the past.

Before I Forget — Interactive Narrative Game

+

An interactive narrative game simulating cognitive decline through evolving environments and UI changes. Built using JavaScript (p5.js), HTML/CSS, Figma, Adobe tools, Gemini, and Git. Designed gameplay systems and interface elements to guide user interaction and reflect emotional experience — as the player progresses, the interface itself begins to fragment, distort, and fade, mirroring the protagonist's deteriorating memory.
